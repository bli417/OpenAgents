## Problem statement
The agent builds useful itineraries, but it does not consistently explain arrival logistics like IC cards, contactless payment expectations, airport transfer choices, or whether any transit pass is worth buying. These are common pain points for first-time travelers.

## Proposed change
Extend the planning flow to include a short logistics section tailored to the destination: recommended payment setup, airport transfer options, local transit card guidance, and a simple statement on whether a pass is worth it for the proposed itinerary.

## Scope
Arrival logistics and final itinerary output
