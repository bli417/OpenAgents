## Problem statement
The current travel agent is strong at day-by-day itinerary building, but it does not explicitly help users choose the best base neighborhood or handle arrival logistics from the airport. These are high-impact early-trip decisions that affect budget, transit time, and trip comfort.

## Proposed change
Extend the agent to ask for arrival airport, hotel preferences, and tolerance for transfers, then recommend 2-3 stay areas with pros/cons and include airport transfer guidance in the final plan. Add simple heuristics for first-time visitors, late arrivals, families, and budget-conscious travelers.

## Scope
Trip planning logic, intake questions, and final itinerary output format
